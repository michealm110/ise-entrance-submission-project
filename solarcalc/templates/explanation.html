What We Did

We developed a simulation tool that models your solar panel system's performance using real-world data and established photovoltaic modeling techniques. Here's how each component contributes to the simulation:

  • Solar Output Calculation: Utilizing the calc_power_output function, we estimate the hourly DC power output of your solar panels based on your location, panel specifications, and orientation. This function leverages the PVGIS Typical Meteorological Year (TMY) data to simulate realistic solar irradiance and temperature conditions. The calculation incorporates factors such as solar position, irradiance on the panel surface, and module temperature to provide an accurate power output estimate.
  • 30-Minute Interval Averaging: To align the simulated solar output with typical energy consumption data intervals, the get_30min_calc_vals function interpolates the hourly power output to 30-minute intervals. This ensures a more precise comparison between your solar generation and energy usage patterns.
  • Financial Projection: The make_financial_projection function models the financial implications of your solar investment over a 25-year period. It calculates cumulative savings from self-consumed solar energy, revenue from excess energy exported to the grid, and compares these against the potential returns from a traditional market investment. The function also accounts for the depreciating value of your solar system over time, providing a comprehensive view of your net financial position.
  • Personalized Data Storage: To securely store and retrieve your simulation inputs, we use a unique identifier system powered by hashids. When you submit your data, it's saved in a lightweight database and linked to a custom alphanumeric code (your hashid). This allows you to return to your personalized results page at any time by visiting the unique URL associated with your hashid — no login or signup required.

What We Give You

  • Live, location-based simulation: We don't just estimate — we use real meteorological data and your Eircode to simulate your exact solar conditions.
  • Smart energy balancing: By comparing your projected solar output to your actual ESB usage, we identify when you'd rely on the grid or export excess.
  • Financial clarity: We show you what your investment would look like against an alternative, so you can compare solar to market-based returns with confidence.

What We Will Improve On

  • Hourly weather-based precision: We're working to incorporate real-time weather forecasts for even more accurate solar output modeling.
  • Battery storage simulation: Soon, you'll be able to add battery capacity to see how storing excess energy affects savings and export potential.
  • Deeper financial breakdowns: Future updates will show payback periods, ROI timelines, and tax incentive modeling for Ireland specifically.
